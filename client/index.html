How to operate:

  • To move WITH KEYBOARD AND MOUSE:
    1. Use the ARROW keys to move.
    2. or press the bar to move in the direction of the mouse.
  • To move on MOBILE:
    Double tap to set direction. Hold to move.
  • To switch between items in your inventory, click on them, or use the number keys through to select the #-th inventory slot.
  • Press to drop the item you are holding
  • Press the key to sprint.
  • To rearrange items in your inventory, simply drag on them.
  • The shadow will adjust based on the location of your mouse.
  • Use your mouse to look around better.

Objectives:

  • Get the highest levels of XP as you can.
  • Get to the top of the leaderboard by kills.
  • DON'T die!
  • Kill the boss mob.
  • How to beat the Boss Mob:
    • The central structure is made up of walls , which can be ascended via the stairs . There are no openings outside until defeating the boss mob .
    • When the boss mob reaches less than 75% health, it summons in 4 enemy Lords. Until these are defeated, the boss mob cannot take damage.
    • After being defeated, the boss mob will respawn in 60 seconds and stairs will appear to allow players (and entities) to go in and out. These will disappear after the boss respawns.

Game mechanics:

  • You start with a sword . Each sword has a health, and will eventually break after enough uses. Swords can deal damage to enemies. A circle indicates when an enemy is within range to deal damage to enemies.
  • Click/tap on enemies to deal damage.
  • Coins give 25 XP.
  • Speed power-ups increase your sprint duration [ + another key] and also give XP.
  • Health power-ups increase your health by 25 points and give you 25 XP.
  • You can go to markets to buy accessories with XP.

WARNINGS:

Look out for dangerous looking people! They want to turn you into a tree!
