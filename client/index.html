How to move:

  • To move:
    1. Use the ARROW keys to move.
    2. or press the bar to move in the direction of the mouse.
  • To switch between items in your inventory, use the number keys through to select the #-th inventory slot.
  • Press to drop the item you are holding
  • The shadow will adjust based on the location of your mouse.
  • Use your mouse to look around better.

Objectives:

  • Get the highest levels of XP as you can.
  • DON'T die!

Game mechanics:

  • You start with a sword . Each sword has a health, and will eventually break after enough uses. Swords can deal damage to enemies. A circle indicates when an enemy is within range to deal damage to enemies.
  • Click on enemies to deal damage.
  • Berries give 1 XP.
  • Speed power-ups increase speed and also give XP.

WARNINGS:

Look out for dangerous looking people! They want to turn you into a tree!
